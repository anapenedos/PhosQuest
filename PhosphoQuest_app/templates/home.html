Welcome to PhosphoQuest!


Begin your quest for Human kinases and their targets, related information and analyse your phospho-proteomics data

PhosphoQuest is a website designed to bring together information and data analysis tools for kinase research. The PhosphoQuest database contains information on kinases, the substrates they phosphorylate, and the drugs that inhibit their activity. These data can be explored through this web app.

For example, you can search by kinase, inhibitors and kinase substrates, using an accession or ID number, or gene/drug names. You can also browse the full contents of the database using our browse pages.

We hope you find this website useful.
